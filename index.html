About

Every year it is a customary in our college Bengal Institute of Technology to celebrate the Fresher’s Day. The aim of conducting this day is to make the juniors feel welcome and to interact with them. The new academic year brings happiness and joy with the arrival of the juniors. To welcome them to the department and make them feel at home, the institute organizes a Fresher’s Day, giving the new comers a step towards their graduation from the schooling days.
“A hundred new faces, but a million dreams” is what this occasion can be best described as. Welcoming these hundred odd a bright faces to an unchartered ocean of knowledge, fear and anxiety are bound to creep into these novice minds.
